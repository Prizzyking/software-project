Entry Requirements

Choose your path – UTME or Direct Entry.

UTME Entry

  • Five O'Level credits (SSCE/WASC) including English, Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry + any other science subject.
  • UTME Subjects: Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry.

Direct Entry

  • Two 'A' Level passes in Physics and Mathematics or Chemistry.
  • NCE/OND (minimum upper credit) in relevant fields.
  • Must satisfy O'Level requirements as above.

🎓 Graduation Requirements

Minimum 144 units (UTME) or 108 units (Direct Entry). Pass all core/elective courses, CGPA ≥ 1.50, and be found worthy in character.

More Information

Transfer Students

Apply through Registrar with transcripts. Admission possible into 200 level maximum. Remedial courses may be prescribed.

Grading & Probation

Pass mark 40%. GPA < 1.50 triggers probation. Two consecutive semesters GPA < 1.50 leads to withdrawal. Grade points: A=5, B=4, C=3, D=2, E=1, F=0.
