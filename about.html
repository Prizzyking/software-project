Our History

From foundation to centre of excellence.

2015

Foundation

Software Engineering programme established under the Department of Physical Sciences, College of Natural and Applied Sciences. First batch of 50 students admitted.

2018

First Graduates

Pioneering cohort graduated with skills meeting NUC benchmarks and global standards. 90% employed within six months.

2022

Independent Department

Became a distinct academic division, offering specialized B.Sc. Software Engineering. Curriculum updated with AI and cloud tracks.

2025

Research Hub

Recognized for cutting-edge research in AI, cloud computing, and cybersecurity. Partnered with 15+ tech companies.

Philosophy & Objectives

Guided by a vision to produce well-rounded, industry-ready graduates.

Broad Foundation

Computing, Science, Engineering, and specialized knowledge in Software Engineering.

Practical Exposure

Hands-on application of Software Engineering to real-world problem solving through labs and internships.

Ethics & Leadership

Training in human behavior, management, and professional responsibility.

Innovation

Encouraging creative thinking and entrepreneurial skills for self-reliance.

Our Mission

Stimulate sustained interest in Software Engineering; train students with solid computing knowledge and skills for effective service; conduct research to advance the state of the art.

Our Vision

To be the leading Department in Research and Teaching towards the economic growth of the nation through ICT.
