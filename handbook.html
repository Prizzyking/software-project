Key Policies at a Glance

Essential rules and guidelines for every student.

Dress Code

Corporate dressing required outside halls. Female: skirts below knees, no transparent/ tight. Male: trousers (not jeans), shirt and tie, no muscle tops/earrings, hair short.

Examination Rules

80% attendance mandatory. No GSM or unauthorized materials. Possession of incriminating material leads to expulsion. Late entry not allowed after 30 mins.

Grading System

Pass mark 40%. GPA < 1.50 triggers probation. Two consecutive semesters GPA < 1.50 → withdrawal. Grade points: A=5, B=4, C=3, D=2, E=1, F=0.

Library & Health

Mandatory library registration. Keep quiet, no food. Health centre registration required; medical reports endorsed by Director.

Registration

Complete within prescribed period. Late registration with penalty up to 4th week. No registration after matriculation.

Misconduct

Sanctions for exam malpractice range from reprimand to expulsion. Offences include smuggling notes, GSM in hall, impersonation.

Important Policies

Probation & Withdrawal

GPA < 1.50 → probation; CGPA < 1.50 in two consecutive semesters → withdrawal.

Course load: Min 15, max 24 units per semester.

Matriculation & Transfer

Compulsory oath taking and signing register.

Transfer only once, maximum 200 level.

Attendance

80% lecture attendance mandatory to sit for exams.

Absence of three weeks without permission leads to withdrawal.
